# Corn Ribs
[Original Recipe](https://www.foodnetwork.com/recipes/ree-drummond/corn-ribs-11367466)

## Equipment

 - Large Baking Sheet
 - Parchment Paper or Silicone Baking Mat

## Ingredients

 - 3 ears of corns
 - Olive oil
 - 1 tbsp. of brown sugar
 - 1 tbsp. of smoked paprika
 - 1 tsp. of garlic powder
 - Kosher salt
 - Black pepper
 - 1/4 cup of mayonnaise
 - 1/4 cup of BBQ sauce
 - 2 tbsp. of brown mustard
 - 1 tbsp. of honey

## Prep

 - Wash the corn and remove any strands
 - Preheat oven to 425 degrees
 - Line baking sheet with parchment paper/silicone baking mat

## Cook

 1. In a small, shallow bowl mix together the brown sugar, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
 2. Use a sharp knife to cut the ears of corn in half, legnthwise. Cut each half widthwise into quarters. Finally, cut each quarter in half, lengthwise. Each ear of corn should produce 8 "ribs".
 3. Lightly brush each rib with olive oil.
 4. Dip each rib into the spice mix until well covered.
 5. Line ribs evenly onto the lined baking sheet.
 6. Bake for 18-20 minutes until the ribs begin to curl and char.
 7. For the sauce, mix together the mayonnaise, BBQ sauce, mustard, and honey. 
 8. Once the ribs are done, remove the pan from the oven and immediately brush each rib with a little bit of sauce.
 9. Transfer the ribs onto a separate dish to serve.
 10. Enjoy!
